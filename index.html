About Me

Hi, I'm Zack, a games programmer from the UK, currently studying at Falmouth University in my second year.

I've always had a fascination with games, taking a strong interest in how they work behind the scenes. This led to me joining the programming club during primary school at the age of 9 which sparked my interest in programming. Since then I have learnt a variety of languages and engines, and have been making games ever since!
