Rattus Machinatus

Made in Unity

Harness your eerie, Pied-Piper croak to memorize and command your swarm of rats. These vermin are not just your minions - they are your weapons. Navigate a treacherous kitchen filled with suspicious and strange mysteries. Use strategy and your rodent army to poison the king's soup.

This project was developed over the course of my second university year with a group consisting of 8 students. I programmed many of the games mechanics, including the Rat's AI, Puzzle Mechanics and Minigames.

Playable through itch.io
